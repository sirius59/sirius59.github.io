My Story

a little bit about Clément

Born in 1998 in France, I live in Bruxelles, Belgium. I studied Sciences in Dunkerque and Paris (France), especially Physics, and specialised in Astrophysics in Liège, Belgium. I'm currently in my way to graduate on a Master degree in Space Sciences. I'm thus writing a master thesis about 'Innovative techniques to find strongly lensed systems'. This work aims at autonomously detect gravitational lenses by using Machine learning techniques.

I really enjoy the Master in Space Sciences. It is meant to be customizable to each student profile while providing theoretical knowledge in Astrophysics as well as practical skills for Space Industry. I mostly learned Astrophysics in my first year of Master and this year I choosed to focus more on applied skills to keep a backdoor open in the non-academic world.

My family would qualify me as a Geek but I am just a curious and passionate guy. In fact, I like Sciences and New Technologies but I also appreciate Art, Music, Sport, Philosophy, Economy, Politics, History and so much more !
